Welcome to Lily

Lily is your personalized, private AI assistant—built just for you. Designed for full customization and privacy-first interaction, Lily helps you manage tasks, access personal data with permission, and assist online or offline.

Get Started

Core Features

  • Fully Customizable: Adapt Lily to your preferences and needs, from appearance to behavior.
  • Access & Edit Your Data: With your permission, view and update personal files, emails, contacts, and more while keeping data local and private.
  • No Usage Limits: Unlimited chats, tasks, and interactions without throttling or extra charges.
  • Screen Interaction: Observe and assist on-screen actions like typing messages or updating tasks directly.
  • Memory & Adaptation: Learns from past interactions to better match your style and anticipate your needs over time.
  • Autonomous Task Handling: Automate and manage your workflows without juggling multiple external tools.
  • Online & Offline Support: Works seamlessly with the internet and provides offline essentials when you’re disconnected.

Target Audience

Lily is perfect for anyone seeking a personal assistant that adapts to their lifestyle—students, professionals, creatives, or anyone who values privacy and customization.

Privacy & Security

Lily is designed with a strict privacy policy: your data stays local and never sold or used outside your region. All interactions are encrypted and stored securely on your device or chosen cloud.

Access & Availability

  • Web Version: Full capabilities online with PWA support; caches crucial data for offline fallback.
  • App Version: Native offline-first mode with extended features like file uploads and persistent memory.
  • Cross-Platform: Connects both versions for a seamless experience when internet is available.
